To tweak the fire ring, change the following values on
Fire and Fire2 under the Player and Player2 parents:

Cosmetics
---------

1) Set SphericalParticleEmitter to Active to see effects

2) Alter Emitter Size to desired amount

Mechanically
------------

On FireRingCollisionHandler on the Player1 and Player2 objects:

1) Change HitDistance variable to desired amount. 
   This is typically slightly lower than the number set for Emitter Size
